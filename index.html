Dragonwake

Dragonwake is set in Valoria, the land once ruled by the old empire of Istaris before the Starfall. Your heirloom, a blackglass star amulet, draws you to the land just as the primordial dragon begins to stir.

A traditional roguelike with a long and detailed quest line, an expansive world map, and lots of replayability. Choose a race and class, then begin your road toward the Sundering Scar. Good luck.

Quick Play Guide

  • Every action is a turn. Move one step, attack, rest, or interact, then nearby enemies act.
  • Press e to interact. Talk to people, open doors, and use exits with the same key.
  • Default controls are built around numpad movement. Dragonwake uses 8-direction movement, so the intended default layout is the numpad.
  • Laptop-friendly layout alternatives are in Options. If you do not want to use numpad movement, switch to one of the alternate control schemes there.
  • Useful keys: esc for menus and back, number-row shortcuts for the in-game menus, x to look, and F1 or ? for help.
  • Early route: buy some travel supplies at the docks, then head inland toward Havenford for the first real town and quest hub.
  • If you are new to roguelikes: play slow, inspect things, retreat from bad fights, and treat survival as progress.

Game Details

  • Large open world map: travel across roads, towns, ruins, towers, dungeons, and blackglass sites as the campaign opens up.
  • Races and classes: choose from races including Human, Dwarf, Elf, Half-Elf, Halfling, Gnome, and Half-Orc, then pair them with classes like Fighter, Ranger, Paladin, Rogue, Cleric, or Wizard.
  • Build your own road: be a dwarf paladin, an elven wizard, a half-orc fighter, or whatever combination suits you.
  • Old-school adventure pressure: combat, traps, hunger, spells, shops, upgrades, and of course dragons.
  • Central mystery: learn what your blackglass star amulet really is, why it drew you to Valoria, and what is waking beneath the Sundering Scar.
